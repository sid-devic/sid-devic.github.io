About

I’m Sid, a first year CS PhD student in the USC Theory Group advised by Vatsal Sharan and Aleksandra Korolova. I am currently interested in the intersection of machine learning theory and algorithmic fairness. I am grateful to be supported by a National Defense Science and Engineering Graduate (NDSEG) Fellowship from Fall 2021.

Here is my CV (March 2021).

I completed my undergraduate degrees in math and CS at The University of Texas at Dallas (UTD), where I explored research in networking, theory, optimization, and reinforcement learning. Along the way, I was extremely fortunate to be advised by these amazing professors: Nick Ruozzi, Jason Jue, Brendan Juba, and Ben Raichel.
